I am Julika Hother, a graphic designer living and working in Hamburg. My work floats between analogue and digital spaces, exploring limits and possible combinations – in an experimental way with a great interest in different media and colourful chaos.


In 2019 I co-founded SAMT Magazine – a platform for student work of the Design Department of the HAW Hamburg. Currently I'm finishing my bachelor degree at the HAW Hamburg.
