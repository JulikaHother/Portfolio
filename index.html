I'm Julika Hother, a graphic designer living and working in Hamburg. My work floats between analogue and digital spaces, exploring limits and possible combinations – in an experimental way with a great interest in different media and colorful chaos.

In 2019 I co-founded SAMT Magazine – a platform for student work of the Design Department of the HAW Hamburg. Currently I'm finishing my bachelor degree at the HAW Hamburg.

Don’t hesitate to contact me for commissions or collaborations.
